# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Buyers and sellers of game accounts (Genshin Impact, Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, Valorant), mostly Philippines-based. Sellers are ID-verified; buyers are anyone signing up to browse and purchase. Most usage is on mobile (360–430px viewports). Many users are not technical — the experience needs to feel trustworthy and low-friction, not flashy for its own sake.

## Product Purpose

ZenLootX is an escrow-based marketplace: payment is held until the buyer confirms the account/items are as described, then released to the seller (or refunded via dispute resolution). Success = buyers and sellers trust the platform enough to transact without anxiety — trust is the product, not just the trade.

## Brand Personality

Dark, premium, calm, minimal. Green accent (`#19C37D`) is a spice, not the main dish — restraint signals premium, not loudness. Tone of all copy: calm, confident, trustworthy, short sentences, not hype-y, no excessive emojis.

## Anti-references

- No bouncy/playful/elastic motion — reads as a consumer toy app, not a money-handling marketplace.
- No fake trust badges, testimonials, partner logos, or fake numbers (hard rule from owner).
- Avoid generic SaaS/AI-slop patterns: gradient text, side-stripe borders, glassmorphism-as-default, cream/sand neutral backgrounds (this product is intentionally near-black/dark).

## Design Principles

- Clean over crowded — when unsure, remove, don't add.
- Calm confidence over hype — every interaction should feel reassuring, never gimmicky, since this product handles people's money.
- Mobile-first always — every flow must work cleanly at 360–430px before anything wider.
- Motion is deliberate and functional — it communicates state (loading, success, error), never decoration for its own sake.
- Only show real trust signals; if a feature isn't built yet, don't display it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Tap targets ≥44px tall on mobile.
- Reduced motion: every animation must have a `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` fallback (crossfade or instant transition) — this product's users are not assumed tech-savvy and motion should never become a barrier.
- Text contrast must meet WCAG AA (≥4.5:1 body, ≥3:1 large text) against the dark backgrounds defined in the design system.
