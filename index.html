How ZenLootX works

Three steps. Your money is safe the whole way.

1

Find a listing

Browse verified listings. Filter by game, type, price, and seller rating. Every listing shows exactly what you're getting.

2

Pay into escrow

Your payment is held by ZenLootX — not released to the seller until you confirm everything is as described.

3

Confirm & done

Once you verify the account or items, approve the release. The seller gets paid. Leave a review and you're done.
