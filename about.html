About ZenLootX

Built for gamers, by gamers.

A safe, escrow-protected marketplace for buying and selling game accounts in the Philippines and Southeast Asia.

Our mission

Trading game accounts has always been risky. Scams are common, buyers have no protection, and sellers have no way to prove trustworthiness. We built ZenLootX to fix that.

Every transaction goes through escrow — the buyer's payment is locked until they confirm the account matches what was listed. No guesswork, no direct transfers, no risk of being ghosted.

What we stand for

  • ✓ Escrow on every order. Funds are only released after the buyer confirms delivery — never before.
  • ✓ Verified sellers only. Sellers go through an identity review before they can list anything.
  • ✓ Human dispute resolution. A real person reviews every disputed order — no automated decisions on your money.
  • ✓ No fake listings or reviews. Everything on the platform must be real. We remove anything that isn't.
