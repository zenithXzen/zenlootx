8. Transactions & Escrow

All transactions on ZenLootX are processed through our escrow system. Here is how it works:

  • Payment held: The buyer's wallet is debited and funds are held in escrow by ZenLootX
  • Seller delivers: The seller transfers the account or item to the buyer via the Platform's messaging system
  • Buyer confirms: The buyer confirms everything is as described and releases the payment
  • Funds released: The net payment (after the platform fee) is transferred to the seller's wallet

Auto-release policy: If the buyer does not confirm delivery or raise a dispute within 72 hours of the purchase date, the escrowed funds will be automatically released to the seller. Buyers are responsible for confirming or disputing within this window.

Platform fee: ZenLootX charges a 5% platform fee on each completed transaction, deducted from the seller's payout. Buyers pay the full listed price. The fee is shown to sellers on the listing page and in their order details before and after purchase. Fees are non-refundable once a transaction is completed.

Transactions are atomic and final. Once a purchase is confirmed and escrow is released, it cannot be reversed except through ZenLootX's official dispute process.
